⚡ Volt Tycoon — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 July 2026 · Developer: Pocketforge Games

English

Volt Tycoon does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • All game progress is saved locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
  • The game requires no account, no registration, and no login.
  • The game does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, files, or any other personal information.
  • The game works fully offline and makes no network requests.
  • If rewarded advertising is added in a future update, this policy will be updated beforehand and the ad provider's data practices will be disclosed here, including any consent required by law (GDPR/CCPA).
  • The game is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Questions? Contact us at doziii@gmail.com.
